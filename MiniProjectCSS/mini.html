How to take care of a watch

Winding the watch
Check if the watch is automatic
  1. Every watch is different, make sure you don't overwind it.
  2. Some watches can only be wound manually.
  3. Automatic watches don't need to be wound, for the most part

There are a few things you should know before winding your watch.

Service
Service centers can be found almost in every city.
  1. Every 3 years you should take your watch for service check.

It is common practice to take your watch to a watchmaker now and then.

Cleaning the watch
Use a brush with some water to clean your watch
  1. It is generally a good idea to wash your watch from time to time, a lot of dead skins and dirt can get trapped in the watch.

Some hot water with a little bit of soap does the trick, for the most part, however, if you want to clean the insides of your watch you should take it to the nearest service center.

Knowing how to set the date proparly
It is very important to not overwind a watch
  1. Generally you dont want to set the date betwen 9pm and 3 am.

Over winding the watch can cause the mainspring to break, or make the watch inaccurate for a long time.

Quartz or automatic
Differences between these two types of watches

Quartz watches are watches that are not powered mechanically but powered with an electrical current ie. battery.
